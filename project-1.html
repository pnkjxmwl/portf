Activity 1

In this Activity we were given a list of movies and we have to choose a movie and review it. And also some answer questions according to movies.

Movie Name: Forest gump

Your Favourite Dialogue and why?

One of the most memorable lines from the movie "Forrest Gump" is "Life is like a box of chocolates, you never know what you're gonna get." This line has become an iconic cultural reference and is often used to describe the unpredictability of life. The reason why this line is so popular and beloved is because it speaks to a universal truth about the human experience. We all encounter unexpected challenges and opportunities in life, and it's up to us to make the most of them. The line also conveys a sense of optimism and hopefulness, suggesting that even in the face of adversity, there is always the possibility of something sweet and enjoyable.

Your Personal review

"Forrest Gump" is a heartwarming and emotional movie that follows the life of a simple and innocent man named Forrest Gump, played brilliantly by Tom Hanks. The film chronicles Forrest's journey as he becomes involved in some of the significant events in American history, from the Vietnam War to the civil rights movement. The movie's exceptional writing and direction by Robert Zemeckis, combined with Hanks' remarkable performance, make this movie a must-see for all movie lovers. It is a touching and inspiring tale that reminds us of the importance of kindness, perseverance, and never giving up on our dreams. Overall, "Forrest Gump" is an excellent movie that captures the human spirit's resilience and the importance of compassion and kindness. It is a timeless classic that continues to inspire and entertain audiences worldwide.

Learning to take away from movie

"Forrest Gump" offers many valuable lessons and takeaways that can be applied to our lives. Here are a few key learnings to take away from the movie: Never give up on your dreams: Despite his low IQ, Forrest never gave up on his dreams and achieved great success in his life. His determination and hard work are an inspiration to us all. Be kind and compassionate: Throughout the movie, Forrest displays kindness and compassion towards others, even in the face of adversity. His actions remind us of the importance of treating others with respect and empathy. Embrace diversity: The movie highlights the importance of embracing diversity and accepting people from all backgrounds and walks of life. Life is unpredictable: The movie's famous line, "Life is like a box of chocolates, you never know what you're gonna get," reminds us that life can be unpredictable, and we should be prepared to adapt and face challenges as they come.

Related concepts you've learned in the classroom

Psychology: Forrest's character displays many traits associated with intellectual disability and autism spectrum disorder. Students can learn about these conditions and how they impact individuals' lives.
Ethics and Morality: The movie raises ethical questions about war, social justice, and personal responsibility. Students can explore these themes and discuss their own beliefs and values.
Cultural Diversity: The movie portrays characters from different cultural and socioeconomic backgrounds, highlighting the importance of cultural diversity and inclusion.
